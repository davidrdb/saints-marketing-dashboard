How to use this tracker

  • Tabs above split by entity — Church, Saints School, Little Saints.
  • Each event shows its 8 phase columns (P0–P7). Cells use ✓ done, 🟢 current (highlighted yellow), 📅 future date, ❓ to confirm.
  • Lead Approver signs off content before publishing. Check before posting.
  • ⚠️ flags in Notes mean a decision is needed. Raise with David.
  • Download Excel (top-right) gives you a multi-tab editable copy — edit in Google Sheets, send back to David for the live update.
